OUR HISTORY

ISSUES

The menace of building collapse and the attended losses and fatalities has been a concern to built environment professionals, regulators and general public. While there are multiple dimensions to the courses for these avoidable disasters , proper and optimal professionalism in civil/structural engineering design and construction supervision is at the heart of the issue. The market is overwhelmed with substandard products, firmness and integrity of regulators and AHJs have been politically diluted, and property developers seem more concerned with the interest rate of their construction loans than the integrity of there structures. These challenges calls for a different kind of structural engineers who understand they are at the center of these variables and responsible for the ultimate stability of our buildings and other infrastructure.

OUR APPROACH

Kkrolands limited came mainly as a result of the above observations, and has many certified professionals in all our spectra of operations. Todays structural engineer needs much more than the knowledge of force resolution, engineering material understanding and 3D structural framing. While these are part of the fundamental knowledge and skillset of a good civil/structural engineer, and probably sufficient to ensuring the developing and developed buildings are safe some decades ago, they are not enough in the current circumstances. The engineer needs to see his/her own contribution to any project as a project on its own and must possess the added project management understanding in quality management, risk management, team management, communication management, resource management, HSE management, and very importantly stakeholder management. This is our approach at KkROLANDs Limited. At KkROLANDs Limited, we see our contribution to project development from the point of the whole rather than from the point of a part. This is the mindset behind our commitment and promotion for all design professionals to migrate towards Building In formation Modelling BIM that would enable seamless integration and harmonization before actual construction than silo delivery. We are also passionate about value engineering, understanding that a client expects optimal combination of stability and affordability.
